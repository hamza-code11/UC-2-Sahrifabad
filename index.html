ABOUT
UNION COUNCIL-2 SARIFABAD

Union Council-2 Sarifabad, TMC Liaquatabad, District West Karachi, is a key local administrative unit dedicated to serving the community at the grassroots level. Our main goal is to provide essential civic services such as clean streets, proper sanitation, street lighting, and other public facilities. We work to address residents’ concerns quickly and effectively while planning and implementing local development projects. UC-2 also acts as a strong link between the people and government institutions, improving communication, transparency, and service delivery for the benefit of all residents.

Our vision is to create a clean, safe, and progressive Sarifabad where every citizen has access to essential facilities, improved infrastructure, and equal opportunities. We aim to ensure that local services meet the needs of the community while promoting transparency, accountability, and active public participation. By doing so, we strive to support sustainable, long-term development that benefits everyone in the area.

Union Council-2 Sarifabad was established to strengthen local governance and address community issues effectively at the neighborhood level. Over the years, UC-2 has carried out numerous initiatives, including sanitation improvements, road and street maintenance, installation of street lights, and resolution of public concerns. These efforts reflect our ongoing commitment to enhancing the quality of life and promoting development in the Sarifabad community.
